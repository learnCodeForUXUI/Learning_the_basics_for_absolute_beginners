FRESH SEAFOOD PASTA
“With a minimum prep time and even shorter cooking time, this mouth-watering dish will be a favorite at the end of a busy day” 
- Bob Smith
Preparation Time Less than 30 mins
Cooking Time Less than 10 mins
Serves 2
Ingredients
1 tbsp olive oil
15g/0.5oz butter
2 spring onions, chopped
2 garlic cloves, chopped
150g/5.5oz mixed fresh seafood
pinch saffron, soaked in 1 tbsp water
150g/5.5oz fresh pasta, such as linguine, cooked according to packet instructions
1 tbsp freshly grated parmesan
Preparation method
Heat the olive oil and butter in a pan, add the spring onions and cook gently for 2-3 minutes.
Add the garlic and fry for another minute. Add the seafood and saffron, along with its soaking liquid, and cook for 2-3 minutes. Stir in the hot cooked pasta and mix well to coat.
To serve, place the seafood pasta into serving dishes and sprinkle over the grated parmesan.
